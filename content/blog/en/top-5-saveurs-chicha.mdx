Choosing a flavor is one of the most personal aspects of the hookah experience. Every smoker develops their own preferences over time, but certain aromas never go out of style. Here is our selection of the five most beloved flavors on the French market.

## 1. Mint — The essential

Year after year, mint remains the most requested flavor. Its natural freshness delivers an incomparable feeling of lightness. It comes in several variations:

- **Icy mint**: Intense and refreshing, ideal in summer or after a hearty meal.
- **Sweet mint**: More subtle, perfect for long sessions with friends.
- **Mint-lemon**: A tangy, fresh blend that wins people over with its balance.

Mint also makes an excellent base for mixes. Add it to any fruity flavor to bring an extra icy dimension.

## 2. Double Apple — The oriental classic

A direct inheritance from Middle Eastern tradition, double apple (or "two apples") is an iconic flavor recognizable among all others. Its anise-like, slightly sweet taste evokes traditional evenings in oriental cafés.

This flavor tends to divide opinion: you either love it or find it too pronounced. For newcomers, we recommend trying it in a mix with a touch of mint to soften the anise notes.

## 3. Watermelon — Summer freshness

Watermelon has established itself as one of the star flavors of recent years. Its fruity, juicy, thirst-quenching profile makes it a perfect choice for laid-back sessions.

- **On its own**: It offers a natural sweetness without being cloying.
- **In a mix**: Pair it with mint or lime for an irresistible tropical cocktail.

Its lightness makes it approachable even for beginners looking for a pleasant first experience.

## 4. Grape — Understated elegance

Grape is an often underrated flavor that nonetheless deserves a place of honor. Its round, velvety aroma recalls the taste of a ripe, sun-drenched grape. Less sweet than watermelon, more subtle than double apple, it appeals to smokers in search of refinement.

Black grape offers deeper, slightly woody notes, while white grape is lighter and more floral. Both pair beautifully with a hint of mint.

## 5. Peach — Fruity sweetness

Peach rounds out our top 5 with its soft, comforting profile. It's a crowd-pleasing flavor that appeals to the majority of smokers. Its aroma evokes summer fruit and brings a touch of nostalgia.

Connoisseur's tip: blend peach with a hint of jasmine for a floral, fruity experience worthy of the finest lounges.

## How to choose your flavor

Choosing a flavor depends on several factors:

- **Your experience**: Start with simple flavors (mint, watermelon) before exploring complex profiles.
- **The moment**: Fresh flavors for summer, warm and spiced flavors for winter.
- **The occasion**: Smoking solo, go with your favorites. In a group, choose crowd-pleasers.
- **The quality of the tobacco**: Premium tobacco renders aromas more faithfully. Don't hesitate to invest in established brands.
